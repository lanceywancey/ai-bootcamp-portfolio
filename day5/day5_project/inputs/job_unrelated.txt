Marketing Specialist — Digital Campaigns
FreshMart Singapore Pte Ltd | Singapore (On-site)

About the Role
FreshMart is Singapore's fastest-growing online grocery delivery platform.
We are looking for a creative and data-driven Marketing Specialist to join
our growth team. You will plan, execute, and measure digital marketing campaigns
to drive customer acquisition and brand awareness.

Responsibilities
- Plan and execute multi-channel digital marketing campaigns (Google Ads,
  Meta, TikTok, email).
- Manage social media content calendars and community engagement.
- Analyse campaign performance using Google Analytics and Meta Business Suite.
- Write copy for email newsletters, landing pages, and social media posts.
- Coordinate with the creative team for campaign assets.
- Report on KPIs: CAC, ROAS, CTR, conversion rate, retention.

Required Skills
- 1–2 years of experience in digital marketing or a related field (fresh
  graduates with relevant internship experience welcome).
- Proficiency in Google Ads and Meta Ads Manager.
- Strong copywriting skills in English; bilingual (Mandarin) is a plus.
- Experience with email marketing platforms (Mailchimp, Klaviyo, or similar).
- Analytical mindset: comfortable reading performance dashboards.
- Creative flair with attention to brand consistency.

Preferred Skills
- Experience with A/B testing and conversion rate optimisation.
- Familiarity with SEO fundamentals.
- Knowledge of customer segmentation and lifecycle marketing.
- Graphic design skills (Canva, Figma, or Adobe suite).

Qualifications
- Diploma or degree in Marketing, Communications, Business, or related field.

About FreshMart
FreshMart delivers fresh groceries from wet markets and supermarkets across
Singapore within 2 hours. We are a Series B startup backed by regional VCs
and serve over 200,000 monthly active customers.

Salary: SGD 3,000 – SGD 4,000/month
Location: Paya Lebar, Singapore (On-site)
