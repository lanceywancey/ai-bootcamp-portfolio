Systems Engineer — Infrastructure & Platform
Phillip Securities Pte Ltd | Singapore (Hybrid) | Entry to Mid Level

About the Role
We are looking for a driven Systems Engineer to join our Infrastructure and Platform team.
You will be responsible for building, maintaining, and scaling internal tools and services
that power our trading platform and back-office operations. This role is a great fit for
recent graduates from computer science or engineering programmes who are eager to work
with real production systems.

Responsibilities
- Design and implement RESTful APIs and microservices using Python and Go.
- Automate infrastructure provisioning with Terraform and Ansible.
- Monitor system health using Prometheus, Grafana, and ELK stack.
- Participate in on-call rotation and incident response.
- Write technical documentation and runbooks.
- Collaborate with software engineers, data engineers, and QA teams.

Required Skills and Experience
- Proficiency in at least one systems programming language: Python, Go, or C++.
- Experience with Linux operating systems and shell scripting (Bash).
- Familiarity with Docker and Kubernetes for containerised workloads.
- Understanding of networking fundamentals: TCP/IP, DNS, load balancing.
- Experience with at least one cloud platform: AWS, GCP, or Azure.
- Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
- Good written and verbal communication in English.

Preferred Skills
- Experience with CI/CD pipelines (GitHub Actions, Jenkins, or equivalent).
- Familiarity with Infrastructure as Code (Terraform, Pulumi).
- Knowledge of distributed systems and consensus algorithms.
- Exposure to financial systems or trading platforms.
- Experience with observability tools (Prometheus, Grafana).

About Phillip Securities
Phillip Securities is one of Singapore's leading financial services firms,
providing brokerage, wealth management, and advisory services to retail and
institutional clients across Asia.

We value continuous learning, technical rigour, and a growth mindset.
Fresh graduates with strong fundamentals and a passion for systems work
are encouraged to apply.

Location: Raffles Place, Singapore (Hybrid — 3 days in office)
Salary: SGD 3,500 – SGD 5,000/month (commensurate with experience)
